## 🏗️ Architecture / How It Works
Here’s the secret sauce behind the Psychic Kung Fu Master Trainer:

1. **Neural Interface Layer**
   The app uses your webcam and microphone (optional) to detect micro-expressions and vocal tone shifts. It translates these into "psychic energy" readings.

2. **Adaptive AI Engine**
   A machine learning model (trained on 10,000+ simulated sparring sessions) predicts your next move and adjusts the difficulty in real time.

3. **Energy Simulation Core**
   Your mental focus (measured via attention algorithms) powers virtual objects in the training arena. Think of it like a psychic weightlifting machine.

4. **Progress Database**
   All your training data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite file. No cloud, no tracking—just you and your gains.

5. **Rendering Pipeline**
   The 3D sparring environment is built with DirectX 12 for smooth, low-latency feedback. Even your *thoughts* feel responsive.
